# Better Horses — Feature Overview

**Better Horses** is a Minecraft mod designed to make horses feel like true long-term companions rather than disposable early-game transport. The mod focuses on fixing vanilla frustrations around horse travel, improving mobility, adding meaningful progression systems, and introducing utility mechanics that make investing in a horse worthwhile.

---

## Ownership System

Once a horse is fully tamed, it becomes **marked as owned** by the player.

Owned horses unlock:

- A **Radial Command Wheel** (opened with **Ctrl + Right Click**)
- A **Horse Whistle** (press **P**) to instantly call your horse to you

### Radial Command Wheel Commands

- **Follow** → Horse follows the player
- **Stay** → Horse remains in place
- **Return Home** → Horse returns to its assigned home location
- **Set Home** → Assign a home location for your horse

---

## Bonding System

Inspired by *Red Dead Redemption*, horses now feature a **Bonding System** that ranges from **0–100**.

Every **20 bond levels** grants stat bonuses:

- Increased movement speed
- Increased jump height

### Bond Gains

- Naming your horse with a **Name Tag** → **+10 Bond**
- Feeding your horse a **Golden Apple** → **+2 Bond**

This system rewards long-term care and makes your horse progressively stronger over time.

---

## Travel & Movement Improvements

### Water Traversal

Horses:

- No longer sink in water
- Move significantly faster across water

---

### Auto-Ride System

Press **W twice quickly** to activate **Auto-Ride Mode**.

While active:

- The horse automatically moves forward
- Movement is controlled using your mouse
- No constant WASD input required

To disable Auto-Ride:

- Press any **W/A/S/D** key to return to manual control

---

### Forest Traversal Fix

Vanilla horses often get stuck in forests due to dense leaf collision.

This mod solves that by making:

- Leaves **passable while mounted**
- Dense forests far easier to travel through

This significantly reduces traversal frustration.

---

## Multiplayer Riding

Horses can now be ridden by **two players at once**.

This allows:

- Cooperative exploration
- Faster multiplayer travel
- Easier transportation of teammates

---

# New Equipment & Items

The mod introduces several new horse-focused items.

---

## Upgraded Saddle

The **Upgraded Saddle** unlocks:

- **5 special equipment slots**
- Additional customization options

It can also be combined with a chest to give horses:

- Their own storage inventory

---

## Hitch Post

The **Hitch Post** allows players to tether their horse to a fixed point.

Features:

- Prevents movement entirely
- Only works on horses owned by the player
- Cannot be used on random wild horses

---

## Medkit

The **Horse Medkit** acts as an emergency survival tool.

When horse health drops below **50%**:

It automatically activates and grants:

- Regeneration
- Instant Health
- Resistance

This gives your horse an additional survival buffer during dangerous situations.

---

## Horse Hooves

Craftable horse hooves provide environmental stability.

Effects:

- Prevent sinking in powdered snow
- Improve movement stability
- Reduce fall damage

---

## Stabilizer

The **Stabilizer** is advanced horse equipment designed for dangerous terrain.

When the horse experiences a major fall:

- Jets of steam activate
- Mechanical wings deploy
- Fall damage is reduced or completely negated

This allows for much smoother cliff traversal and protects horses from accidental deaths.

---

# Mod Philosophy

Better Horses aims to solve a simple problem:

**Vanilla horses are fun early on—but quickly become obsolete.**

This mod transforms horses into:

- Reliable travel companions
- Stronger exploration tools
- More practical multiplayer mounts
- Long-term investments worth caring about

Instead of replacing horses with overpowered mechanics, Better Horses enhances what already makes them fun—while removing the frustrations that stop players from using them.
